A Simple Health Advisor

Our app promotes a healthy, active and tobacco-free lifestyle.

We do this in three ways:

Willpower Battery

We ask you about what you're feeling and then we warn you when you need to be more vigilant. We also email you at the end of the week with personalised advice.

Accountability Buddies

Choose one goal for the week and try to stick to it. If you want, get paired up with someone from around the world and tell them about your progress.

Browse/Submit Routines

Submit your own morning/night routine on our website and get feedback from others. Browse the routines of top performers to see what they do differently.

When you try to do something new, your willpower gets depleted. Like a battery. But the good news is, it can also be built up and trained.

Changing a habit is easier if someone else is going through the same thing with you.

Studies show that you can only change one habit at a time. Neural connections in the brain take a minimum of 60 days to be formed.

Prevention is Better than Cure.

According to the World Health Organization (WHO), about 55 million people died worldwide in 2011, two thirds of this group from non-communicable diseases, including cancer, diabetes, and chronic cardiovascular and lung diseases.

We use science to help you make smarter, better habits. We know when you are likely to relapse and send you a personalised email with the latest scientific discoveries.
